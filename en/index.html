About Us

Welcome to uOttawa Esports, your home for esports on and off of campus. Join us for both casual and competitive gaming at the University of Ottawa! The competitive teams that we offer are listed with their respective contact on the Teams page.

A Community for All

Not only do we offer a competitive environment for the competitive gamer, but also a friendly and safe environment for anyone wanting to pick up a new game, to find players to play with, or even to get away from the books!

Funding Groups and Partners

The gamers at uOttawa Esports would like to extend a thank you to our funding groups and partners including Campus Vibez uOttawa (CVUO) and Red Bull.
